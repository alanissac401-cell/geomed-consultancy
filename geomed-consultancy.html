Why Georgia?

The Smart Choice for
International Students

Georgia has emerged as one of the world's top study-abroad destinations — affordable, safe, and globally recognised.

🏛️

WHO & MCI / NMC Recognised Degrees

Medical degrees from Georgian universities are accepted for licensing exams in India (FMGE/NExT), Gulf countries, UK, and beyond.

💰

Affordable Tuition & Living Costs

MBBS programs start from $4,500/year. Monthly living costs average $300–500. Huge savings vs. private colleges in India or the Gulf.

🌍

English-Medium Programs

All partner universities offer full English-medium teaching for MBBS, BBA, Engineering and more — no language barrier.

🛂

Simple Visa & Residence Process

Student TRC (Temporary Residence Card) is straightforward. GeoMed handles the paperwork end-to-end.

🏥

Clinical Exposure from Year 1

Universities like European University operate their own hospitals, giving students hands-on clinical training early.

🔒

Safe, Student-Friendly City

Tbilisi consistently ranks among the safest capitals in Europe. Vibrant international student community.

Admission Process

How We Get You Admitted

From first enquiry to landing in Tbilisi — GeoMed guides you through every step.

1

Free Consultation

Contact us on WhatsApp or fill the form. We assess your profile and recommend the best university and program.

2

Document Preparation

We provide a clear checklist — 10th / 12th marksheets, passport, photos, medical certificate. We verify everything.

3

University Application

We submit your application directly to the university's admission office on your behalf and secure your offer letter.

4

Visa & Travel

We guide you through the Georgian student visa / e-visa process. Citizens of many countries get visa-on-arrival or e-visa.

5

Arrival Support

Airport pickup, hostel / apartment arrangement, university orientation — we're here when you land in Tbilisi.

6

TRC & Insurance

We assist with Temporary Residence Card registration and health insurance enrollment so you're covered from day one.
